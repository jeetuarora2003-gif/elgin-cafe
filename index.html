Our Heritage

A place you end up staying longer than planned.

Tucked inside Amritsar's Cantonment — a calm city apart from the city — Elgin is a space built for unhurried evenings. Emerald and candlelight. Wood-fired bread and a well-made drink. The kind of company that makes hours disappear.

A brand extension of the Elgin hospitality group, the cafe bridges day-bistro and evening bar without apology — doing both with the same obsessive attention to detail.
