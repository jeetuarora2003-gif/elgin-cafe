Our Heritage

A Cultural Hub of Diverse Flavours

Nestled in Amritsar’s sprawling Cantonment area, Elgin Cafe is an attempt to communicate the tale of the city’s rich cultural heritage. Accented with our signature emerald green and gold, the decor exudes an impression of old-world grandeur that is synonymous with the Elgin group.

Combining the best of a day-bistro and a trendy bar by night, we offer a space where guests feel surrounded by nature, flavours, and conversations.
