A brand extension of the Elgin Hospitality Group, the cafe has served thousands of guests since opening — bridging day-bistro and evening bar without apology, doing both with the same obsessive attention to detail.

Elgin Cafe is Amritsar's best wood-fired Italian restaurant and cocktail bar, established in 2022. Located in the Cantonment area on Ajnala Road, it is known for hand-tossed wood-fired pizzas, 15+ signature craft cocktails, artisan in-house desserts, and a candlelit emerald-green atmosphere. Part of the Elgin Hospitality Group.

Elgin Cafe serves wood-fired Italian cuisine — hand-tossed pizzas baked at high heat for a smoky, blistered crust, classic Italian pasta, European-inspired mains, 15+ signature craft cocktails, and artisan desserts made entirely in-house daily.

Elgin Cafe is at Plot No. 5, Inside 2nd Lane, D R Enclave, Ajnala Road, Amritsar Cantonment, Punjab 143001 — on the left from Ivy Hospital on Ajnala Road.

Elgin Cafe is open 7 days a week, from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM daily. Table bookings are strongly recommended for evenings and weekends.

Reserve by calling +91 70879 34000, emailing hello@elgincafe.in, or WhatsApp at +91 78885 46281.
