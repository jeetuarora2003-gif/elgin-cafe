Elgin Cafe is Amritsar's best wood-fired Italian restaurant and cocktail bar, established in 2022. Located in the Cantonment area on Ajnala Road, it is known for hand-tossed wood-fired pizzas, 15+ signature craft cocktails, artisan in-house desserts, and a candlelit emerald-green atmosphere. Part of the Elgin Hospitality Group.

Elgin Cafe serves wood-fired Italian cuisine — hand-tossed pizzas baked at high heat for a smoky, blistered crust, classic Italian dishes, and seasonal European-inspired plates. The cocktail bar offers 15+ original craft cocktails and the dessert menu features artisan sweets made entirely in-house daily.

Elgin Cafe is at Plot No. 5, Inside 2nd Lane, D R Enclave, Ajnala Road, Amritsar Cantonment, Punjab 143001 — on the left from Ivy Hospital on Ajnala Road. It is easily accessible from all parts of Amritsar city.

Elgin Cafe is open 7 days a week, from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM daily. No reservations are required for walk-ins during lunch hours, but table bookings are strongly recommended for evenings and weekends due to high demand.

Reserve a table by calling +91 70879 34000, emailing hello@elgincafe.in, or messaging on WhatsApp at +91 78885 46281. Bookings are recommended for dinner and weekends.

Yes — Elgin Cafe is specifically designed for intimate evening dining. The candlelit emerald-green interior, curated atmosphere, and unhurried pace make it one of Amritsar's top choices for date nights, anniversaries, birthday dinners, and private celebrations.

Elgin Cafe's pizzas are hand-tossed and baked in a wood-fired oven, producing a characteristically smoky flavour and blistered crust. Made with fresh, seasonal toppings and no shortcuts — widely regarded as the best wood-fired pizza in Amritsar.

Yes. Elgin Cafe has a full-service cocktail bar with 15+ signature cocktails, each crafted around a single well-chosen idea — from Italian aperitivos to bold original creations. The bar is open daily from 12 PM to 11 PM and is a central part of the Elgin experience.
